OUR STORY

Started by a kid from Philly. Run by family ever since.

Thomas “TJ” Smith learned to toss dough in front of a pizza-shop window in Philadelphia. Thirty-seven years later, he’s still in this kitchen on Gulf Boulevard — he and Kim, cooking his family’s recipes, some more than a century old, seven nights a week.

Nothing comes from a freezer. Everything is sautéed, baked, and plated to order — the way it would be at a Sunday table in the old country, with the Gulf breeze drifting in.

THE KITCHEN

If it’s on your plate, it was made from scratch today

San Marzano tomatoes, fresh basil, and garlic perfuming the air — that’s the foundation. On top of it: hand-picked local grouper, tender veal, juicy steaks, and signature pizzas from the brick oven.

Traditional Italian meets tropical seafood — dishes you’d find in Naples, made with what the Gulf gave us this morning.
